Important Parameters in iwconfig:

ESSID: Name of the Wi-Fi network.
Mode: Managed (client mode) or Master (AP mode).
Frequency: Wi-Fi frequency in GHz.
Bit Rate: Maximum data transfer rate.
Tx-Power: Transmission power in dBm.
Access Point: MAC address of the connected Wi-Fi router.

Important Parameters in ifconfig:
  
inet addr: IP address assigned to the device.
Bcast: Broadcast address for the network.
Mask: Subnet mask.
MTU: Maximum Transmission Unit (default 1500 bytes).
HWaddr: MAC address of the interface.
UP/RUNNING: If the network is active.
